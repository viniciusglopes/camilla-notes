## TOPICO 1: Adjetivos (Adjectives)

Adjetivos descrevem pessoas, animais ou coisas. Em ingles, vem ANTES do substantivo.

Exemplos:
- a big panda
- a fast cheetah
- a beautiful butterfly

### Lista de Adjetivos Importantes

**Opostos:**

|----------|-------------|--------|-------------|
| tall | alto | short | baixo |

| old | velho | new/young | novo/jovem |
| long | longo | short | curto |
| strong | forte | weak | fraco |
| fast | rapido | slow | lento |
| happy | feliz | sad | triste |
| beautiful | bonito | ugly | feio |

### Exercicio - Adjetivos

Complete com o adjetivo correto:

1. An elephant is very ___. (big/small) → **big**
2. A cheetah is very ___. (slow/fast) → **fast**
3. A tortoise is very ___. (fast/slow) → **slow**
4. My friend loves sports. He is very ___. (shy/sporty) → **sporty**
5. She always talks in class. She is very ___. (shy/talkative) → **talkative**
6. He goes to the library every day. He is very ___. (naughty/clever) → **clever**
7. The lion is ___ and ___. → **strong and brave**
8. My grandmother is very ___. She always helps me. → **kind**

## TOPICO 2: Comparativos (Comparative Adjectives) ⭐ MAIS IMPORTANTE

Usamos comparativos para COMPARAR duas pessoas, animais ou coisas.

**Estrutura:** NOME 1 + is/are + COMPARATIVO + than + NOME 2

### Regras para formar o comparativo

#### Grupo 1 - Adjetivos curtos (1 silaba) → adicione -ER
| Adjetivo | Comparativo |
|----------|-------------|
| tall | taller |
| short | shorter |
| old | older |
| long | longer |
| smart | smarter |
| fast | faster |
| nice | nicer |
| cold | colder |

Exemplo: "Ana is taller than Pedro."

#### Grupo 2 - CVC (consoante+vogal+consoante) → DOBRA ultima consoante + ER
| Adjetivo | Comparativo |
|----------|-------------|
| big | bigger |
| hot | hotter |
| fat | fatter |
| thin | thinner |
| sad | sadder |

Exemplo: "Summer is hotter than winter."

#### Grupo 3 - Terminados em consoante + Y → troca Y por IER
| Adjetivo | Comparativo |
|----------|-------------|
| happy | happier |
| lazy | lazier |
| busy | busier |
| funny | funnier |
| heavy | heavier |
| healthy | healthier |
| easy | easier |
| early | earlier |
| sunny | sunnier |
| naughty | naughtier |

Exemplo: "Dogs are lazier than cats."

#### Grupo 4 - Adjetivos longos (2+ silabas) → MORE + adjetivo
| Adjetivo | Comparativo |
|----------|-------------|
| beautiful | more beautiful |
| artistic | more artistic |
| talkative | more talkative |
| hardworking | more hardworking |
| friendly | more friendly |

Exemplo: "She is more artistic than her uncle."

#### Grupo 5 - Irregulares (decorar!)
| Adjetivo | Comparativo |
|----------|-------------|
| good | better |
| bad | worse |
| far | further |

Exemplo: "Pizza is better than salad."

### Exercicios - Comparativos

**Parte A - Escreva o comparativo:**

1. tall → **taller**
2. big → **bigger**
3. happy → **happier**
4. beautiful → **more beautiful**
5. good → **better**
6. hot → **hotter**
7. lazy → **lazier**
8. fast → **faster**
9. bad → **worse**
10. thin → **thinner**
11. funny → **funnier**
12. old → **older**

**Parte B - Complete as frases:**

1. Elephants are ___ (big) than dogs. → **bigger**
2. Cheetahs are ___ (fast) than turtles. → **faster**
3. My sister is ___ (tall) than me. → **taller**
4. Summer is ___ (hot) than winter. → **hotter**
5. Cities are ___ (busy) than villages. → **busier**
6. My hair is ___ (long) than your hair. → **longer**
7. She is ___ (clever) than her brother. → **cleverer**
8. This book is ___ (good) than that one. → **better**
9. Cats are ___ (lazy) than dogs. → **lazier**
10. My mom is ___ (hardworking) than my dad. → **more hardworking**

**Parte C - Coloque em ordem:**

1. than / winter / hotter / summer / is → **Summer is hotter than winter.**
2. cities / than / busier / are / villages → **Cities are busier than villages.**
3. is / older / than / Sydney / Rome → **Rome is older than Sydney.**
4. than / my / is / longer / sister's / hair / my → **My sister's hair is longer than my hair.**
5. are / stronger / than / wolves / dogs → **Wolves are stronger than dogs.**
6. is / English / easier / than / Japanese → **English is easier than Japanese.**

**Parte D - Forme perguntas e responda:**

1. you / kinder / brother / are / your / than / ?
   → **Are you kinder than your brother? Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.**
2. uncle / your / is / more artistic / you / than / ?
   → **Is your uncle more artistic than you? Yes, he is. / No, he isn't.**
3. sister / your / naughtier / brother / your / than / is / ?
   → **Is your sister naughtier than your brother? Yes, she is. / No, she isn't.**
4. mom / your / funnier / sister / your / than / is / ?
   → **Is your mom funnier than your sister? Yes, she is. / No, she isn't.**

## RESUMO FINAL - Checklist

1. **ADJETIVOS** - saber o significado dos principais
2. **COMPARATIVOS** - a parte mais importante!
   - Curtos: + ER (tall → taller)
   - CVC: dobra + ER (big → bigger)
   - Consoante + Y: troca Y por IER (happy → happier)
   - Longos: MORE + adjetivo (more beautiful)
   - Irregulares: good→better, bad→worse, far→further
   - Sempre usar THAN para comparar!
3. **INTERPRETACAO** - ler com atencao, voltar ao texto
4. **COMPLETAR FRASES** - usar o contexto

**DICA DE OURO:** Conte as silabas!
- 1 silaba = + ER
- 2 silabas terminando em Y = troca Y por IER
- 2+ silabas = MORE + adjetivo
